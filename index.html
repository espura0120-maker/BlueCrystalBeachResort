About the Resort

A Signature
Island Experience

Blue Crystal Beach Resort sits above the shimmering waters of Palangan Bay, offering a refined escape where hand-carved Nara wood furnishings, marble floors, and open-air terraces frame every breathtaking view.

Whether you're seeking a romantic retreat, a family holiday, or a peaceful solo escape — our 15 air-conditioned ocean-facing suites and warm Filipino hospitality ensure every moment is unforgettable.
